Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 July 2026

How Far/Much is designed so that we know nothing about you. The app has no account, no sign-in, no server, no analytics and no advertising. This policy explains what the app does with data on your device.

Health & fitness data

With your permission, How Far/Much reads workouts from Apple Health: activity type, date and time, duration, distance, active energy and average heart rate. It uses this data solely to calculate the totals, trends and exports you see in the app.

  • The app is read-only — it never writes to, changes or deletes anything in Apple Health.
  • All calculations happen on your iPhone. Your Health data is never transmitted anywhere.
  • You can revoke access at any time in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

Information we collect

None. We operate no servers and receive no data from the app — not your workouts, not your settings, not even crash reports or usage statistics.

Sharing & export

The app can create a summary image, a text summary or a CSV file of your workouts — but only when you tap Share, and the content goes only where you choose to send it (Messages, Mail, Files and so on). Nothing is shared automatically.

Data retention & deletion

How Far/Much stores only your preferences (units, filters, duplicate settings) on your device. Deleting the app removes them. Your workouts remain safely in Apple Health, untouched, because the app never had its own copy.

Third parties

There are none. No third-party SDKs, no data processors, no partners.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the effective date revised before any change takes effect.
