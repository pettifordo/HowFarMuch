Your workouts,
added up.

How Far/Much reads the workouts you've already recorded in Apple Health and answers four simple questions — how far, how long, how much, how many — for today, this week, month, year or all time.

Get support

📏 Four honest numbers

Distance, time, calories and workout count, totalled per activity — running, cycling, swimming, strength and 40+ more.

📈 Trends that tell the truth

Drill into any activity to see whether you're getting faster, going longer and burning more — or the opposite, gently.

🔁 Duplicate detection

Phone and Watch both recorded the same run? A third-party app too? Duplicates are spotted and counted once, with an adjustable sensitivity.

📤 Share & export

Share your totals as a good-looking card or plain text, and export every workout — including average heart rate — as a CSV.

🎛️ Your rules

Filter activities, exclude workout types, hide sub-minute accidental starts, and choose miles or kilometres.

🔒 Private by design

No account, no cloud, no analytics. How Far/Much reads Apple Health on your iPhone and nothing ever leaves it.
